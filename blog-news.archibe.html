About Indeat

Indeat started in 2014 in Munich, Germany and since 2015 belongs to one of the world's largest food delivery companies, Delivery Hero. Our customers get access to restaurant food, food, books, flowers and everything else they need in their everyday lives - with fast and smooth delivery! Through us, our partners gain access to more and more customers, which increases their sales and reach.
